Documentation
=============

`Telegram-Bot-Discussion` is Python-framework for Telegram-bot making.

Main idea of `Telegram-Bot-Discussion` is simplification of dialogues-functions which save context between `Telegram-user` and `Telegram-bot` in requests-responses flow. 

Developer in its `Discussion` just construct `Dialogue` logic by `Replicas` sequence and expected responses.
